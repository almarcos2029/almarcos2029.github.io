Spinach Pizza: the simple and homemade recipe

Steps on how to cook a spinach pizza.

Sept. 20, 2025

List of ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Baby Spinach
  • Mozzarella
  • Feta cheese
  • Garlic
  • Virgin olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

Tips before you start cooking:

  1. Use your fingers to stretch the dough, don’t use a rolling pin (Step 1).
  2. Don’t put raw spinach on a pizza. You want to sautee your baby spinach (Step 2).
  3. Place the rack in the lowest position close to the heating element.
  4. Preheat the oven.
  5. Always be careful and ask for assistance, you may burn your hand.
  6. Bake your pizza on a hot pizza stone or pizza steel. You can also use a baking tray.
  7. Use parchment paper and a pizza peel or the back of a baking sheet to transfer your pizza to the hot stone. This helps, especially if you use a sticky high-hydration dough.

Steps:

  1. First step, start by making the dough.
  2. Saute’ the garlic in some olive oil, add the spinach a handful at a time and toss gently until wilted and still bright green.
  3. Season with salt and pepper and set aside.
  4. When the dough is ready, stretch it gently into a rectangular shape (optional).
  5. Top with mozzarella, crumbled feta, then scatter the sautéed spinach mixture over the top.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 15 minutes until golden and crusty.
  7. Serve with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil, freshly ground black pepper or red pepper flakes (or both).

Enjoy your homemade spinach pizza!
